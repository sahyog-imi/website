About SAHYOG

Where Academic Rigour Meets
Real-World Markets

SAHYOG is the Student Managed Investment Fund (SMIF) of the International Management Institute (IMI), Delhi — a completely student-run fund constituted as a Section-8 not-for-profit entity.

This is not a trading simulation. SAHYOG manages real capital under real institutional governance, with formal accountability at every step. Every trade executed under pre-approved governance controls. Every report published under faculty oversight. Every process documented and audited monthly.

"The best way to train the next generation of investment professionals is not to teach them about markets in a classroom, but to place them inside a functioning investment organization."

— Dr. Monika Chopra

🏛️

Institutional Governance

Faculty oversight, monthly audits, pre-trade approvals, and Investment Committee sign-offs — every layer of governance mirrors a professional buy-side investment firm.

🔬

Full Research Process

Every thesis goes through fundamental analysis, technical validation, formal Investment Memo preparation, and Investment Committee approval before any trade is executed.

📐

Quantitative Capability

SAHYOG's quant team builds and backtests algorithmic strategies using charting tools, scripting workflows, and real historical NSE and BSE price data.

Our mission is to cultivate disciplined, ethical, and research-driven investors by managing capital responsibly, generating long-term welfare, and serving as a bridge between academic rigor and real-world financial markets.

— SAHYOG Mission Statement

Organizational Structure

Three Verticals,
One Integrated Fund

Each vertical holds a distinct mandate and a defined set of responsibilities, functioning together as a single investment organisation.

01 / VERTICAL
📈

Finance

The intellectual engine of SAHYOG. Responsible for identifying opportunities, building the full analytical case — business model, financials, valuation, growth potential — presenting the Investment Memo to the IC, executing trades, and tracking positions to exit, with technical analysis supporting entry and exit timing.

Equity Research Investment Memo Technical Analysis Portfolio Mgmt
02 / VERTICAL
📣

Marketing

Manages SAHYOG's external presence — LinkedIn content, alumni network outreach, campus recruitment campaigns, and institutional communication with IMI faculty. Every report, presentation, and post is reviewed to meet SAHYOG's brand standards. Also organises guest speaker talks and inter-college competitions.

03 / VERTICAL
⚖️

HR & Compliance

Keeps SAHYOG running as a real organisation. Handles full recruitment cycles, structured performance reviews, SOP documentation, compliance with legal obligations, monthly audit coordination, and credential management — while maintaining a healthy and functional work culture.

How Can I Join?

SAHYOG recruits from IMI Delhi's full-time programmes at the start of each academic term — for all three verticals. We look for intellectual curiosity, willingness to work within structured processes, and a genuine commitment to the work. Shortlisted candidates submit a written application and attend a structured interview.
